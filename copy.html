Sunrise is building an army of young people to make climate change an urgent priority across America, end the corrupting influence of fossil fuel executives on our politics, and elect leaders who stand up for the health and wellbeing of all people. We are ordinary young people who are scared about what the climate crisis means for the people and places we love. Public opinion is already with us - if we unite by the millions we can turn this into political power and reclaim our democracy.

Here's how to get involved with the local Sunrise NYC hub:

  • Join our hub-specific Slack workspace for day-to-day communications. This is where we do most of our communication.
  • Subscribe to our contact list, where we share news and events.
  • Add our Google Calendar to see upcoming events. You need to be logged into Google to add that calendar via the link. If you don't have a Google account, you can still view the calendar here.
  • Let us know if you are interesting in meeting with a current hub member to learn more about Sunrise NYC and how you can get involved! We highly suggest it - it's a great way to meet another Sunriser, talk about your interests, and figure out the best ways for you to get plugged into our work. The best way to contact us is via Slack.

For more information about what we do, visit our national website here and follow our NYC hub on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram to stay in the loop on all things Green New Deal and our fight for a just and livable future.
